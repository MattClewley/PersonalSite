Development & QA

Polygon Software was a site developed with Drupal 7. The site was handed over to me and a coworker via a Webflow export. We put the site files on the server, downloaded Drupal on it, and then made the content dynamic for our client, Polygon Software. The pieces of content available are basic pages, articles, solutions, services, testimonials, and webforms. We made sure all modules were up to date and downloaded ones that were most useful to the site.

We created several webforms such as: Contact, Request A Demo, and Support webforms. These all have their own pages they are displayed on. The site has also been QA-ed, and is SEO friendly. This project took place in July 2019.

Primary technologies used: PHP, Drupal 7, SQL, Javascript, CSS, HTML.
