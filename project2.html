Pickup PG

Development, UX, UI, & Lead Role

This project took place over the course of the last month in the Fall Semester of 2019 at the University of Maryland. This project consisted of, including myself, 5 people on a team working to produce a web application that solved an information problem within the community. Our focus for this project was to allow users to find places in Prince Georges County where cleanup efforts occured, when they occured, and what organizations were involved/how to get in contact with them. We aimed to solve the environmental issue of littering within PG County by making this information more easily accessible.


Multiple pages were developed including: Home Page, Get Involved, Where We Clean, About Us, and Documentation. The Where We Clean page is the most advanced and important page as it contains the Leaflet map with the data points and information. Mulitple filters were used: Fiscal Year and Council District. We sped the overall page speed up by not having any data points loaded up upon loading the page.

Primary technologies used: Node, ExpressJS, Javascript, Heroku, CSS, HTML.
